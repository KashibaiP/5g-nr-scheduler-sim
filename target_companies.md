## Critical Skills Employment Permit (CSEP) — Quick Notes

- You **qualify** as a software engineer (on the eligible occupations list)
- Minimum salary threshold: **€34,000** (well below market — not a barrier)
- Employer must apply on your behalf — mention it as "I will require CSEP sponsorship" in applications
- Processing time: ~6–8 weeks currently
- Most Tier 1 companies above have done this many times — not a blocker

**Key doc**: https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/what-we-do/workplace-and-skills/employment-permits/
